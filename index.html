I am the sort of person who is always building something.

In my time at Northwestern, that meant building student organizations. Whatever my role, that meant working with my fellow students to organize events of every type, from slam poetry workshops to kite flying days.

When I graduated, I spent a year tutoring high school students in math and volunteering with organizations working to build a more inclusive and democratic society, like Invisible 2 Invincible and Reclaim Chicago.

I came to realize, though, that while organizing is powerful, there's a shortage of people with technical skills in much of this community-building work. So I decided to retool, and headed to Dev Bootcamp to learn web development skills.

If you are looking for a web developer for a public interest project, please contact me by email (sunjaydk@gmail.com).
